Frosty Strawberry Smoothie:

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups Organic Strawberries
  • 3/4 cup of Yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon Lemon Juice
  • 2 Tbsp Honey
  • 1 cup Ice Cubes

Method:

  1. Cut up your Strawberries
  2. Add in the Strawberries, Yogurt, Lemon Juice, Honey, and Ice Cubes all into the blender.
  3. Blend all ingredients for about two minutes.
  4. Enjoy!!!
